Our Story

Universe Exports have been registered in April 2018. We offering import and export goods with quality assurance. We procuring and supplying materials from farmers', Farmer Producing Companies (FPCs) and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) from all over Maharashtra and Certified Manufacturing companies. We excluded the chain of middlemen and supplying goods direct from farmers, these things keep our time and money. We are concentrating on providing goods at fewer prices than the market with quality assurance. We are running this business with standard certifications recommended by the Government of India. We are a domestic supplier as well as a merchant exporter. We also passed all the official procedures and certified by the International Standard Organization’s 9001:2015 ISO Certification.
